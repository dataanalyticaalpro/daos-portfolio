## Day 6 Reflection – Business Process Before Data

Today I learned that data should never be my starting point when solving a business problem. Every dataset is created because a business process produced events that were recorded as data.

A business event is the real activity that occurs in the business, while data is simply the recorded representation of that activity. Understanding the business process helps me ask better questions and prevents me from making incorrect assumptions from the dataset.

I also learned to think beyond the data by identifying the business processes that could contribute to a problem before writing any SQL. This approach allows me to build hypotheses first and then use data as evidence to confirm or reject them.

### Key Takeaway

Business processes create reality.
Data records reality.
Analysis explains reality.
Business decisions improve reality.
