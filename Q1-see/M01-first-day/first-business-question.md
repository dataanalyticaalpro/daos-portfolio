# What is data?

Data is a collection of facts, observations, or records about people, events, or business activities. By itself, data has little meaning until it is analyzed to answer a business question.

---

# Why do businesses collect data?

Businesses collect data to:

- Understand customer behavior
- Measure business performance
- Identify problems and opportunities
- Make informed decisions
- Improve products and services
- Increase revenue while reducing costs

---

# Five business questions from a sales dataset

1. Which products generate the highest revenue?
2. Which customers purchase most frequently?
3. Which days of the week record the highest sales?
4. Which products are underperforming?
5. Which stores consistently outperform the others?
