# Day 8 – Business Questions Before Data

## Summary

Today's lesson focused on understanding the relationship between business goals, business problems, and business questions before analyzing any data.

### Key Learnings

- A business goal is the desired outcome the organization wants to achieve.
- A business problem is an obstacle preventing the business from achieving its goal.
- Business questions help identify the root cause of the business problem.
- Analysts should understand the business context before opening any dataset.
- Data records business events but does not create business problems.

### The Three Whys

Before beginning any analysis, ask:

1. Why is this analysis needed?
2. Why is it needed now?
3. Why this report instead of another?

### Key Takeaway

Business understanding comes before data analysis. Asking the right business questions ensures that analysis supports business decisions instead of merely producing reports.
