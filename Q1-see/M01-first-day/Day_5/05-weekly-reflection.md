# DAOS - Month 1 Week 1 Day 5

## Weekly Transformation

This week taught me to think like an analyst instead of someone simply looking at data.

### Key Lessons
- Data records business activities.
- Business problems exist before data problems.
- Understanding business processes comes before analysis.
- Never clean data without understanding why it exists.
- Questions and hypotheses should guide every investigation.

### Biggest Insight
The reason spreadsheets often feel overwhelming is not because of the spreadsheet itself, but because I do not yet fully understand the business process that produced the data. Once the business context becomes clear, the data begins to tell a meaningful story.

### Evidence of Growth
Compared to the beginning of the week, I now naturally ask questions such as:
- Compared to what?
- What business process produced this data?
- What hypothesis can I test?
- What evidence do I need before making a recommendation?

I am beginning to think like an analyst before thinking like a SQL user.
