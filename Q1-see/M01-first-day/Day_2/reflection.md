# Day 2 Reflection

## What I learned

Data tells a story.

Before changing the story, I must understand why it was written that way.

My job is not simply to clean data.

My job is to protect the truth.

## What I built

I built a reasoning process for inspecting a dataset before modifying it.

## Evidence

- Completed a dataset assessment.
- Explained why duplicate records should not be deleted immediately.
- Identified business risks of deleting valid data.

## Independence Gained

I now inspect data before attempting to answer a business question.
